Ethan Chern

I am a PhD student at the Generative AI Research Lab (GAIR), Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Pengfei Liu. My current research interests are in video foundation models, real-time video generation, and world modeling.

Previously, I worked on LLM alignment, factuality, evaluation, and multimodal learning. I hold a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the Language Technologies Institute, School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. During my Master's years at CMU, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Graham Neubig and Dr. Pengfei Liu on the factuality of LLMs and summarization models.

I also had the opportunity to collaborate with Prof. Yu Tsao on multi-modal speech enhancement and voice conversion. During my undergraduate years, I worked with Prof. Ching-Yi Lai on quantum error correction and information theory.
